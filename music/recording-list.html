"Tre Giorni Son Che Nina - Evan Troendle

"Tre Giorni Son Che Nina" is a lyrical masterpiece that encapsulates the essence of unrequited love in the heart of a passionate soul. Composed by the illustrious Italian composer, Giovanni Battista Pergolesi, this aria is a jewel of Baroque music, capturing the timeless emotions of longing and despair.
The title itself, which translates to "Three Days Have Passed Since Nina," sets the stage for a poignant narrative. In just a few minutes of musical enchantment, Pergolesi weaves a tale of love's ache and melancholy. The delicate, cascading melodies entwine with Nina's wistful lyrics, creating an atmosphere of profound vulnerability.
As the notes dance gracefully through the air, listeners are transported to a world of yearning, where the longing for a beloved is palpable. Pergolesi's masterful composition is a testament to his ability to convey the most profound emotions through music, making "Tre Giorni Son Che Nina" a timeless gem that resonates with the deepest chambers of the heart. It serves as a reminder that love, in all its forms, is a universal experience that transcends time and place, leaving an indelible mark on the human soul.

"Sure on This Shining Night - Evan Troendle

"Sure on This Shining Night" by Samuel Barber is a musical reverie that enchants the senses and stirs the soul. This art song, with text by the celebrated American poet James Agee, stands as a radiant testament to the beauty of the night and the profound emotions it can evoke.
Barber's composition is a lyrical masterpiece that transports the listener to a serene, moonlit dreamscape. The music is a delicate balance of poignant harmonies and tender melodies, creating an atmosphere of tranquility and introspection. As the vocal line weaves through the richly textured piano accompaniment, it evokes a sense of wonder and awe in the face of the tranquil night.
The lyrics, penned by Agee, are a celebration of the quietude and serenity that can be found in the nighttime hours. They speak to the profound sense of peace that descends upon the world when the stars twinkle in the sky, and the cares of the day fade into the background. Through Agee's poetic verses and Barber's exquisite music, "Sure on This Shining Night" captures the essence of a timeless, almost mystical connection between the human spirit and the nocturnal world.
Barber's composition is a testament to his skill in setting poetry to music, creating a piece that resonates deeply with anyone who has ever felt the profound stillness and beauty of a peaceful night. "Sure on This Shining Night" stands as a shimmering gem in the world of art song, a piece that continues to captivate and move audiences with its ethereal beauty and evocative power.

"The Brigand of the High Seas (2010) - Composed by Evan Troendle (b. 1992) - Performed by James Ellis

"The Brigand of the High Seas" by Evan Troendle, a talented composer born in 1992, is a captivating and innovative violoncello composition that marries contrasting musical elements with remarkable finesse. This piece, which earned the prestigious Iowa Composer's Forum annual composer's competition prize in the secondary school age category in 2010, showcases the extraordinary talent and creativity of its young composer.
Troendle's composition is a striking fusion of genres, blending the aggressive energy of metalcore with the elegance of classical music, all while evoking the stormy and adventurous spirit of piracy on the high seas. This unique combination results in a musical journey that is both intense and evocative.
Performed by the accomplished cellist James Ellis, this piece comes to life with passion and precision. Ellis, the principal cellist of Orchestra Iowa, brings out the full spectrum of emotions embedded within the composition, from the thunderous and aggressive passages reminiscent of a tempestuous sea to the moments of quiet elegance that mirror the stillness of the ocean before a storm.
"The Brigand of the High Seas" is a testament to Evan Troendle's creative genius and his ability to push the boundaries of musical expression. It offers listeners a thrilling and immersive experience that transcends traditional genres, transporting them to a world of high-seas adventure and musical exploration.
This composition not only showcases the promising talent of a young composer but also serves as a reminder of the boundless possibilities that music offers, where genres can collide and new, exciting landscapes of sound can be charted.
